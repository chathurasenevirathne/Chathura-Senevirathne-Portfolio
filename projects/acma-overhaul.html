Packaging Machine Overhaul & Spare Parts Localization

Description

Major overhaul and life-extension of ACMA 325 Cartoner and ACMA 741 Wrapper while localizing critical spare parts.

Key Actions

  • Redesigned tablet pusher assembly.
  • Manufactured critical components locally.
  • Refurbished gears, shafts, bearings and chains.
  • Eliminated wear and reliability issues.

Key Results

  • Reduced cost from LKR 18 Mn to LKR 2 Mn.
  • 88% cost reduction.
  • Extended machine life by approximately 5 years.
  • Improved reliability and availability.

Skills Gained

Mechanical Engineering • Reliability Engineering • Machine Overhaul • Cost Optimization • Asset Management
