Engineering Loss Reduction Program

Description

Plant-wide reliability improvement program focused on reducing engineering-related manufacturing losses through proactive maintenance and structured problem solving.

Key Actions

  • Performed Pareto analysis of breakdown data.
  • Identified critical failure hotspots.
  • Strengthened PM, BM and Predictive Maintenance governance.
  • Established weekly RCA reviews.
  • Conducted technical knowledge-sharing sessions.

Key Results

  • Reduced engineering losses from 5.0% to 3.2%.
  • Improved uptime and reliability.
  • Established RCA and CAPA culture.
  • Improved maintenance governance.

Skills Gained

Reliability Engineering • RCA • Data Analysis • Maintenance Management • Leadership
